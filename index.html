About Us

With over 30 years of experience in manufacturing food seasonings, flavorings, and colorings, Pointing Technologies is a new arm of Damah Group, expanding into machinery trading and innovative food processing solutions.

Pointing Technologies is commited to providing cutting edges food machinery solutions that enhance the growth and operational efficiency of our clients in the snacks, vegetarians/soya protein, and pet food sector.

At Pointing Technologies, we combined our rich heritage and innovative spirit to deliver unparrallelled solutions in the food industry. Our strategic partnerships and comprehensive approach enabled us to meet the evolving needs of our clients, ensuring their success and satisfactions.

Our Production Lines

Features:
This production line uses potato starch and corn starch to create a variety of popular twice-extruded snacks with unique shapes, like crispy peas, shells, and more. It features advanced techniques, high automation, and one-step steaming, extruding, and shaping without a boiler, resulting in crispy, delicious snacks.

Features:
Our production line utilizes raw materials such as corn starch, flour, and potato starch to manufacture a variety of 3D compound puff foods, including "bugles”, "web crisps" and "cartoon stereo-crisps."

Features:
The breakfast cereal and corn flakes production line employs grains as its fundamental raw material to manufacture nutritious and convenient puffed foods available in various shapes, including granules, flakes, and animal shapes.

Features:
The production line for texturized soy protein/texturized vegetable protein utilizes low-temperature soybean meal and peanut meal as its primary ingredients, resulting in a diverse range of textured soybean protein foods available in various shapes, including columns, squares, slices, thin strips, particles, and more.

Features:
In the production line for puff/core-filling snacks, the extruder employs an advanced co-extrusion technique capable of creating two distinct snack foods with varying textures and flavours. This versatile line can automatically produce popular snacks like puffed fragrant nuggets and core-filling rolls. Moreover, it can manufacture nutritional powder, breakfast cereals, and a range of other puffed snack foods simply by altering the die and auxiliary equipment.

Features:
The instant rice production line uses rice flour as the main ingredient and can incorporate vitamins and minerals as needed. Through extrusion and drying, it forms rice-like grains that can be cooked in 5-8 minutes, enhancing the rice's micronutrient content and stability.

Features:
The pet food manufacturing line utilizes raw materials such as flour, corn meal, soybean meal, meat meal, fish meal, and more to create pet food distinguished by its innovative shape, distinctive flavor and coloring, scientifically balanced nutritional composition, and improved digestibility.
